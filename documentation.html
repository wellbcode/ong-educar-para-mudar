Forms

This section describes the forms used throughout the NGO website, including their purpose, fields, validations, and best practices.

ğŸ“¬ Contact Form

Purpose: Allow visitors to reach out to the NGO for questions, suggestions, or partnerships.

  • Fields: Full name (required), Email (required), Subject (optional), Message (required)
  • Validations: Valid email format, minimum 20 characters for message, name must not contain numbers
  • Behavior: Displays success message after submission; may be integrated with email or backend

ğŸ¤ Volunteer Form

Purpose: Register individuals interested in volunteering for NGO projects.

  • Fields: Full name (required), Email (required), Phone (optional), Area of interest (required), Availability (required), Additional message (optional)
  • Validations: Valid email format, phone number with Brazilian mask, required fields must be filled
  • Behavior: Data may be stored in a database or sent via email; may include consent for data usage (LGPD)

ğŸ’° Donation Form

Purpose: Facilitate donations from visitors.

  • Fields: Name (optional), Donation amount (required), Payment method (required), Comment (optional)
  • Validations: Minimum donation of R$10.00, required selection of payment method (Pix, credit card, boleto)
  • Behavior: Redirects to payment gateway or displays QR code; may include automatic receipt

âœ… Best Practices

  • Use of label and placeholder for accessibility
  • Visual feedback for invalid fields
  • Clear and concise error messages
  • Semantic grouping with <fieldset> and <legend>
  • Mobile-friendly layout

ğŸ” Privacy Considerations

All forms comply with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (LGPD):

  • Minimal data collection
  • Explicit consent when required
  • Clear purpose for each data field

ğŸ“¦ Future Integrations (Suggestions)

  • Integration with Google Sheets or Airtable for storing responses
  • Automatic confirmation emails
  • Admin dashboard to view submissions
